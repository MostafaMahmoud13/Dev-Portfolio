The Experience

A five-act voyage
into the infinite

01

Pre-Launch Immersion

Three days at our coastal preparation facility. Centrifuge training, zero-g simulation, and a curated programme of meditation, cuisine, and anticipation.

02

Launch Day Ceremony

A private dawn ceremony at the pad. Witness the sunrise over the ocean from the gantry. The last quiet moment before everything changes.

03

Orbital Ascent

Eight minutes of pure acceleration. The sky transitions from blue to violet to the infinite black of space. Earth curves below you for the first time.

04

Life in Microgravity

Twelve to thirty days aboard the station. Float freely through panoramic cupola windows. Watch sixteen sunrises every day. Dine on Michelin-calibre cuisine prepared in zero-g.

05

Return & Integration

Re-entry is controlled, serene, and breathtaking. Landing is followed by three days of integration — the world looks different now. You are different now.
